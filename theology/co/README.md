# Theology — Co

This folder develops the thesis that institutional Christianity has operated from a deliberately corrupted source — one that erased the feminine divine, slandered its primary witness, and built a power structure Jesus explicitly opposed. The recovery project reads the full tradition with the erasures restored, finding a theology of unconditional love that is structurally complete in a way orthodoxy never managed.

---

## Documents

**[1 - summary.md](1%20-%20summary.md)** — *The Suppressed Theology: A Reconstructed Reading of the Feminine Divine, the Gospel Tradition, and the Cosmic Joke at the Heart of Christianity*
A systematic reconstruction of what was erased: Asherah and Sophia in the pre-Deuteronomic tradition; Mary as the embodied Holy Spirit rather than passive vessel; the Trinity reconceived as Father / feminine Spirit (Mary) / incarnate Son; Jesus's ministry as a comprehensive demonstration of how masculine love operates correctly in relation to the feminine. Traces the pattern of erasure from the 7th century BCE through 591 CE and argues that the actual transmission of the faith has been feminine all along — hiding in plain sight in the text the institution handed down.

**[2 - love story.md](2%20-%20love%20story.md)** — *A Love Story: The Bible from the Beginning*
The Bible read as a continuous love story — the divine pursuing relationship with humanity across the whole arc of the tradition, with the suppressed feminine woven throughout. Traces the story from creation through the kenotic demonstration of the Cross to the recovery of what was there from the beginning. The story resolves to six words: "I'm sorry." "It's ok. Together."

## Reading Order

Either document can serve as entry point. **1 - summary** is the more detailed scholarly reconstruction; **2 - love story** is the narrative form of the same thesis. They are complementary readings of the same underlying claim.
