# Bible Arc

This folder reads the biblical narrative through the radical-kenosis framework: agency, proxy, cost, sacrifice, displacement, and love.

## Contents

- [1 - creation.md](1%20-%20creation.md) through [5 - other.md](5%20-%20other.md) move from creation through pre-kingship material.
- [6 - kingdom.md](6%20-%20kingdom.md), [7 - temple.md](7%20-%20temple.md), [8 - prophets.md](8%20-%20prophets.md), and [9 - exile.md](9%20-%20exile.md) track the rise and failure of centralized proxy structures.
- [10 - second temple.md](10%20-%20second%20temple.md) sets the final pre-Jesus context.
- [11 - jesus.md](11%20-%20jesus.md) and [12 - jesus death resurrection.md](12%20-%20jesus%20death%20resurrection.md) bring the arc to Jesus, the Cross, and resurrection.

## Reading Path

Read the numbered files in order. The sequence is cumulative.
