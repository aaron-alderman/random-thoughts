# Bible Arc

This folder reads the biblical narrative through the radical-kenosis framework: agency, proxy, cost, sacrifice, displacement, and love.

## Contents

- `1 - creation.md` through `5 - other.md` move from creation through pre-kingship material.
- `6 - kingdom.md`, `7 - temple.md`, `8 - prophets.md`, and `9 - exile.md` track the rise and failure of centralized proxy structures.
- `10 - second temple.md` sets the final pre-Jesus context.
- `11 - jesus.md` and `12 - jesus death resurrection.md` bring the arc to Jesus, the Cross, and resurrection.

## Reading Path

Read the numbered files in order. The sequence is cumulative.
