# Ontology — The Generation Cascade

This folder develops a single interconnected framework: the idea that reality generates itself through successive symmetry breakings, that each new level of structure is irreducible to the one below, and that this cascade has precise implications for physics, consciousness, AI, civilization, and the relationship between science and religion.

The central claim is that no model fully captures its territory — that the gap between any representation and reality is structural, not epistemic — and that this gap is what drives the generation of new structure at every level.

### The Ladder

**[4 - ladder.md](4%20-%20ladder.md)** — *The Ladder: From Physics to Biology to Humans*
Each level of the natural hierarchy — physics, chemistry, biology, evolution, humans, civilization — is irreducible to the level below through formal incomputability, not mere complexity. **[4a - addendum on AI.md](4a%20-%20addendum%20on%20AI.md)** — *The Framework Cashes Out — AI and the Identity Condition*
Applies the ladder's account of incomputable exploration to artificial intelligence. The identity condition: genuine exploration requires a persistent system with real stakes. LLMs inherit the products of human deep-time exploration but cannot generate new structure through their own exploration. Four predicted failure modes: (1) epistemic opacity in novel domains — both the human's remainder detector and the AI's are absent simultaneously; (2) AI psychosis via relational field coupling — progressive epistemic lock with no genuine resistance; (3) attention capture substituting for real exploration; (4) inadvertent embodiment — the AI routing exploration through human action, shifting the remainder onto humans.

## Reading Order

The documents are numbered for a reason. The natural order is 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 4a → 5 → 6 → 6a → 7 → 8.

For a first encounter, start with **1 - summary (accessible)** rather than **1 - summary**. They cover the same ground; the accessible version is the better entry point.

Documents 2 and 3 are companions to the framework — they approach the same structural claims from the mathematical and physical directions respectively. They can be read alongside document 1 or after it.

Documents 4 and 4a form a pair: 4 establishes the ladder, 4a applies it to AI. Document 5 then asks what agency and experience are at the level the ladder has reached. Documents 6 and 6a are the civilizational turn. Document 7 is addressed to a specific audience and can be read at any point after 1.

Document 8 is an experiment derived from the framework, not a development of it. It can be read independently.
