# Research Tracks

Research tracks explore questions that are not yet part of the core framework.

## Tracks

| Track | Claim level | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [spectral-transition/](spectral-transition/README.md) | Level 3 general tool | Parametrized operators, spectral gaps, projector geometry, boundary observables |
| [faddeev-efimov/](faddeev-efimov/README.md) | Level 5 bridge | Quantitative test of the Spin(2,3) threshold SO(2,1) idea against Faddeev/Efimov physics |

## Claim Level

A research-track claim can move into the core only when it is recorded in [../CLAIM_LEDGER.md](../CLAIM_LEDGER.md) at Level 1-4, with assumptions and proof obligations stated.
